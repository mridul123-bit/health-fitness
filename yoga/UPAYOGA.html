UPAYOGA

“Upa-yoga is a good start towards a more complete physical life. Those who wish to explore a deeper dimension can go into yoga.” – Sadhguru Upa-Yoga is a simple yet powerful system of exercise that activates the joints, muscles, and energy system. Based on a sophisticated understanding of the body’s mechanics, Upa-Yoga dispels inertia in the body’s energy and brings ease to the whole system. It relieves physical stress and tiredness.

Benefits

Relieves physical stress and tiredness. Strengthens the joints and muscles. Rejuvenates the body after periods of inactivity. Negates the effects of jetlag and long travel. Pre-Requisites No previous experience with yoga is required. Suitable for children over 7 years of age and also the elderly.
